Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FunnyBusy-Ness is a personal pin scheduler. It posts product images from a Redbubble store to a Pinterest account owned by the same person running the app.

The app accesses one Pinterest account — the owner's. It reads board IDs to know where to post, and it creates pins. That's it.

The app collects no data from other Pinterest users. It stores no Pinterest data. It does not track, profile, or share anything with third parties.
